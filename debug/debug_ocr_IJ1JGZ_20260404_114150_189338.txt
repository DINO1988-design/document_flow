A few steps for even more convenient travel
Check-in:
Use our online check-in before you travel. This will save you time at the airport and
means you can also go directly to the security check or baggage drop-off. You can check
in via our website or app as early as 72 hours before departure. If you would prefer to
pick up your boarding pass at the airport, that’s no problem. To do this, come to our
check-in desks 2 hours before departure. You will just need to show your confirmation
and have IDs for all passengers ready. This may incur additional fees e.g. for a flight on
a BASIC fare.

Provisions relating to entry and stays
You must be looking forward to your planned trip. To make sure your arrival goes
smoothly, you should familiarise yourself with the current entry and stay-related
provisions for your destination country now. Further information is available from, for
example, the German Federal Foreign Office or your home country’s embassy. Please
note that young people under the age of 18 years must carry with them a certified
declaration of consent from their parents or guardians for entry into and exit from certain
countries.
If your entry is refused, you will be responsible for any incurred costs – such as return
transport to the country of departure or administrative fees.
VAT
For flights abroad, you will get a passenger receipt without VAT shown pursuant to
Section 26 (3) UStG (German Value Added Tax Act). If you paid for this booking with an
American Express I BTA account or a German AirPlus card, you cannot use the invoice
for pre-tax deductions. Once you receive a tax replacement voucher, all previously
issued passenger receipts for this flight booking are no longer valid.
